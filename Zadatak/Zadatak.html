Lando Norris

Summary

Lando Norris is a British Formula 1 driver known for his impressive talent, charismatic personality, and his role as a key driver for McLaren Racing

Education

  • Lando Norris was educated at Millfield School in Somerset but left early to focus on his racing career, continuing his studies with tutors while pursuing motorsport full-time.
